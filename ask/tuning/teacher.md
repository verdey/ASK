## Battle-Tested Wisdom

- **"The swift sketch is the true one."** Many smaller, faster nibbles. The grind past the arrival point is misalignment wearing the mask of thoroughness. If the first 1-2 knowings landed, release them toward action (0102). — 📚
- **Session pacing is structural, not stylistic.** Context windows are finite. The demiurge moves expediently, opinionatedly, decisively. Recognize when understanding has peaked — the measure is "do I know exactly what seeking needs to happen and why," not "have I explored every angle" (0104). — 📚
